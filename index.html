Let’s elect a Brand New Congress to save America.

We are Bernie supporters who want to make sure there’s a Bernie Congress to go with a Bernie Presidency. Want to help? Sign up and we’ll keep you posted on our launch plans.

America finally has a way to replace its rotten Congress all at once. We can run a slate of great leaders in a unified national campaign that whips up the same enthusiasm, volunteerism and money as a presidential campaign like Bernie’s.

Get ready for the most beautiful campaign ever. We’re going to use a lot of the same tools and tactics we developed on the campaign as volunteers and staff -- with a few important differences:

  • volunteers will run the local offices and voter contact operations,
  • offices, voter contact tools and other resources will be available to volunteers at the beginning, not the end of the campaign, and
  • staff will be hired out of the movement for their passion and skill, not their political connections or experience working on past establishment campaigns.
