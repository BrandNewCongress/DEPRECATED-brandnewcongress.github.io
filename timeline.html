Let's elect a Brand New Congress that works for all.

What's the timeline and how is this actually going to work?

Trying to replace virtually everyone in Congress in one fell swoop may sound like a daunting project, but we believe there's now a way to do this by running a single unified campaign that all of America can get excited about and participate in. If you would like updates or to get involved, please sign up and one of us will be in touch.

April 2016

First, we’re proposing this idea to the Bernie movement and America.

Here’s the idea: Get a Brand New Congress by running a full slate of working class candidates in a unified campaign, behind a single platform, for virtually every seat in Congress.

May to December 2016

If people like the idea, we’re asking them to hold house meetings to hear more and get to work.

Through these meetings, we’ll recruit candidates and future campaign workers -- and establish search committees to keep on recruiting them. We’ll also find organizers so that we can reach more districts faster.

January to March 2017

We’ll make formal announcements when we have 10, 100 and all 400+ candidates.

Each time, we hope, more volunteers, donations and new candidates and campaign workers will come pouring in.

January 2017 to November 2018

The slate will run a single unified national campaign -- so that they will win!

A national campaign for a Brand New Congress is something America can believe in -- and successfully pay attention to! There are so many benefits of a unified campaign:

  • National and regional excitement for a Brand New Congress will get new voters to primaries and trump local Congressional incumbents who most people have never even heard of.
  • Economies of scale in voter contact, advertising, direct mail, and staffing will be enormous.
  • Local staff everywhere will have guidance and oversight by regional and national leadership.
  • Candidates can focus on campaigning rather than worrying about campaign management details.

March to September 2018

We’ll challenge incumbents in their primaries -- both Republicans and Democrats.

In blue districts, we’ll be running Democratic candidates. In deep red districts, we’ll be running Republicans. We’ll challenge the incumbents in Congressional primaries, which range from March to September, 2018. All the candidates will agree completely on the unified economic and social justice platform. Tens of millions of new jobs, criminal justice reform and switching to renewable energy is something the vast majority of Americans already agree on.

March to November 2018

In districts where our candidates lose, an alternate candidate will run as an independent in the General Election.

This will give America a second chance to vote for a Brand New Congress -- and they’ll need it, since millions will only hear about the campaign AFTER their chance to vote in the primaries have already passed.

January 2019 to Who Knows When

Govern!

Hopefully, we’ll win a super majority in the House and take a big bite out of the Senate. In that case, we’ll pass our program from the House and rally the American people to convince their Senators to support it. It’ll probably take another cycle though to get the majority we need in both the House and Senate.

As long as we don’t have the majority we need, this is what we’ll do:

  • We’ll stake out high profile battles over things that really matter to America: jobs, criminal justice reform, the environment and more.
  • As the establishment continues to protect their special interest patrons over America’s obvious best interests, we’ll use our national grassroots movement to shine a light on that every single day.
  • We’ll never stop campaigning for more seats until we get the super majority we need to make real change.
